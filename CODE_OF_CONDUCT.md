# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in technical discussions.
- Assume good intent and focus on facts, code, and outcomes.
- Accept feedback gracefully and revise proposals based on evidence.
- Help keep discussions on-topic and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions
that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to: `dev@dragonscale.ai`

Include links/screenshots/context so maintainers can investigate quickly.
All reports will be handled as confidentially as possible.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
code review discussions, and other community interactions related to this repository.
